Executive

Bakare Taofeek

Real Estate Manager
He graduated from University of Ilorin in 2002 with Bsc (Hons) Economics and MBA from Ladoke Akintola University of Technology, Ogbomosho. He began his working career with Sparklight Property Development Company Limited in 2005 where he spent few months and then joined Stallion Home Savings and Loan Limited. While in Stallion, He worked in different departments of the bank and left after 9 years as the Head of Risk Management of the bank. He later worked with Safetrust Mortgage Bank Limited as Deputy Manager, Business Development before joining Crescentpillars Investments Limited as Real Estate Manager.
