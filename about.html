About Our Company

Who We Are?

Crescentpillars Investments Limited is a dynamic financial intermediaries services company founded on the 12th of May 2011. We operate to provide a wide range of financial intermediaries services to a diversified customer base that includes individuals, corporate entities and other financial institutions. Our focus is to deliver exceptional services to the satisfaction of our clients while operating a highly professional organization.

We have a team of dedicated, experienced and highly qualified professionals from diverse disciplines who work diligently to achieve our set objectives in an ethical and proficient manner. Crescentpillars Investments has engaged in several financial activities which involves high volume of transactions in the area of fund transfers which includes bills settlement abroad, payment for goods and services, payment for tuition fee/medical bills Abroad, bureau de change amongst many others. The company has grown to become one of the leading providers of retail and wholesale foreign exchange services to businesses, individual and corporate clients.

We offer our clients expert financial advisory services on investments. Our team of professionals are always available to provide expert guidance on wise investments decisions and present various opportunities where investible funds could be channeled for a reasonable yield. We leverage on our in-depth knowledge of various markets, with an array of qualified professionals to profitably manage investments. Our innovative strategies are aimed at growing our client’s investments with utmost security.
